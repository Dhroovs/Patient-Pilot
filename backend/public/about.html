About MedTranslate AI

“Turning Complex Prescriptions into Simple, Understandable Language.”

🚨 Problem

Medical prescriptions are often difficult to understand due to complex terms and unclear handwriting. Many patients get confused about dosage, timing, and purpose of medicines, which can lead to serious health risks.

💡 Our Solution

MedTranslate AI uses advanced AI to convert prescriptions into simple language. Users can upload an image or text, and our system explains everything clearly — what to take, when to take it, and why.

⚙️ How It Works

  • Upload prescription (image/text)
  • OCR extracts text
  • AI processes and understands data
  • Vector Search retrieves relevant information
  • Output is generated in simple language

🚀 Key Features

  • Prescription Simplification
  • OCR-based Text Extraction
  • AI-powered Explanation (RAG)
  • Accurate Vector Search
  • User-Friendly Interface

🛠️ Tech Stack

Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Backend: Node.js
Database: MongoDB Atlas
Vector Search: MongoDB Vector Search
Embeddings: Xenova Transformers
AI Model: Gemini
OCR: Tesseract

🌍 Impact

MedTranslate AI improves patient understanding, reduces medication errors, and bridges the communication gap between doctors and patients.

🎯 Future Enhancements

  • Multi-language support
  • Voice-based explanation
